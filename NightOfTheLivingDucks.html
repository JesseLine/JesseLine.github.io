Night of the Living Ducks

Night of the Living Ducks is a two person CO-OP tower defense game where the players are tasked with defending Mother Duck from waves of zombie duck. I was part of the five person team which designed and developed this game in a 1.5 month time period for our EECS 494 course at the University of Michigan.

We used Unity and C# to develop this game and used Jira and Github to collaborate and track our progress. My main task in this project was to develop the GameController functionality, which includes the wave spawning (with a wave customization function, audio management, medkit spawning, and End Game control. Another one of my tasks was to create the tutorial for the Bathtub scene, which involved making cutscenes to help guide the player through the game mechanics.
